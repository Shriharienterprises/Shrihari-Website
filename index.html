About Shri Hari Enterprises

Shri Hari Enterprises is a trusted name in the commercial automobile electrical components industry. We specialize in providing a wide range of high-quality, reliable, and affordable products such as horns, relays, bulbs, wiring solutions, and switches — all designed to meet the rugged needs of the transport sector.

What sets us apart is our strong commitment to offering top-grade components at very cost-effective prices, without compromising on quality. We understand the challenges of maintaining vehicles in demanding environments, which is why we ensure our products deliver long-term performance and value.

With a reputation built on trust, consistency, and service, we support our clients through seamless procurement — both offline and through our dedicated inventory and ordering software. Whether you're a small fleet operator or a large logistics provider, Shri Hari Enterprises is here to simplify your maintenance operations and reduce your procurement costs.
